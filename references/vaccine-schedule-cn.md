### Combination Vaccine Logic

When parents opt for self-paid combo vaccines, adjust the NIP doses to avoid duplication:

- If using **五联 (Pentaxim)**: Skip free DTaP doses at 3m/4m/5m. Pentaxim covers DTaP+IPV+Hib.
  - Give Pentaxim at 2m, 3m, 4m, 18m
  - Give free NIP bOPV at 4m, 4yr only
- If using **六联 (Hexaxim)**: Covers DTaP+IPV+Hib+HepB
  - Give at 2m, 4m, 6m
  - HepB birth dose still required separately
  - Add Hib booster at 18m (or use Pentaxim at 18m)
- **Do not duplicate** same antigen — e.g., if HepB given via Hexaxim, do not give free HepB at 1m/6m

## Part 3: Age-Based Catch-Up Reference

For children who missed early vaccines:

| Child's Age | Catch-Up Notes |
|-------------|----------------|
| < 12 months | Can start all vaccines on time; Rotavirus MUST start by 15 weeks (Rotarix) |
| 12–24 months | PCV: 1-2 catch-up doses; Hib: 1 dose if 12-15m; Rotavirus: TOO LATE |
| 2–5 years | HepA 2-dose series; PCV-PPSV23 if PCV never started; EV71 still applicable |
| 5+ years | HPV planning; annual flu; DTaP booster at 6yr (free DT) |
| 9+ years | HPV vaccination recommended to start |

## Part 4: Clinic-Specific Notes (China)

- **社区卫生服务中心 (Community Health Center)**: Free NIP vaccines. Need to register (建档) at local hukou address or residence permit area.
- **国际医疗机构 (International clinics)**: Offer imported brands (Pentaxim, Hexaxim, Prevnar, etc.) — self-paid. SinoUnited, Raffles, Parkway, etc.
- **儿童医院疫苗门诊**: Usually carry more self-paid options than community centers.
- Appointment (预约) usually required. Book 1–2 weeks in advance.
- **Bring to clinic**: 疫苗本 (vaccine booklet), 出生医学证明 or 户口本, parent ID
